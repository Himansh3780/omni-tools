What is the Fake FaceTime Simulator?

The Fake FaceTime Generator is a free online tool designed for creators, pranksters, and social media enthusiasts. It allows you to simulate a realistic iOS video call interface directly in your browser. Whether you want to create a viral TikTok POV video where a celebrity calls you, or prank your friends by showing them a fake incoming call, this tool provides a hyper-realistic environment to do so.

How to Use This Tool

  • Step 1: Enter the name of the person "calling" you (e.g., Drake, Billie Eilish).
  • Step 2: Upload a video file (MP4) of that person. This will play as the main video feed once you answer.
  • Step 3: Upload a photo. This creates the realistic blurred background for the "Incoming Call" screen.
  • Step 4: Click "Start Call". You will see the incoming call screen first. Click the green button to answer.
  • Step 5: Enable your webcam to appear in the Picture-in-Picture (PIP) box, just like a real call.

Features & Capabilities

Our simulator is built with precision to match the modern iOS 17 aesthetic. Features include:

  • Flip Camera Support: Toggle your webcam view (PIP) horizontally to match your preference.
  • Active Timer: A functional call timer that counts up in real-time.
  • Toggle UI: Tap the screen to hide buttons for clean screen recording.
  • Smart Blur: Automatically generates the frosted glass effect for incoming calls.
