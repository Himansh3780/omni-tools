Free Receipt Generator for Landlords & Freelancers

Lost the receipt book? Need to send a quick proof of payment to a tenant or client? Our Free Online Receipt Maker creates a clean, professional "thermal-style" receipt in seconds.

Unlike complicated invoice software, this tool is designed for speed. It is perfect for one-off transactions like rent payments, used car sales, or freelance deposits.

When to use a Receipt vs. an Invoice?

  • Invoice: Sent before payment to request money. (Use our Invoice Generator for this).
  • Receipt: Given after payment to prove money was received.

Common Uses

  • Rent Receipts: Landlords can quickly generate PDF proof for tenants paying cash.
  • Craigslist/Facebook Sales: Selling a laptop or car? Print a receipt to transfer ownership officially.
  • Petty Cash: Small businesses can document cash expenses for tax time.
