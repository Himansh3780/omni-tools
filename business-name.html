How to Choose the Perfect Business Name

Your business name is the first thing customers see. It needs to be memorable, easy to spell, and relevant to your industry. Our AI Name Generator uses algorithms to combine popular prefixes, suffixes, and industry terms to create thousands of unique brandable names in seconds.

Types of Brand Names

  • Descriptive: Tells you exactly what they do (e.g., General Motors).
  • Evocative: Suggests a feeling or metaphor (e.g., Nike, Amazon).
  • Invented: Completely made-up words (e.g., Kodak, Xerox). These are great for trademarking.

Checking Domain Availability

Once you find a name you love, the next step is to secure the .com domain. If the .com is taken, consider modern extensions like .io (for tech), .co, or .store.
