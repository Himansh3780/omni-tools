Why You Need a Strong Password

Hackers use "Brute Force" attacks to guess passwords. A simple 8-character password can be cracked in less than 1 second. A secure, random 16-character password would take a supercomputer trillions of years to crack.

What makes a password secure?

  • Length: At least 12 characters (16+ is recommended).
  • Complexity: Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols.
  • Unpredictability: Avoid common words, birthdays, or names.
