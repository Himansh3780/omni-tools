Whether you are a real estate agent, a SaaS sales representative, or an affiliate marketer, knowing your exact payout is crucial. Our Commission Calculator helps you instantly determine how much you will earn from a sale based on your agreed-upon percentage.

How is Commission Calculated?

The standard formula for calculating sales commission is:

Commission = Sale Price × (Commission Rate ÷ 100)

For example, if you sell a house for $500,000 and your rate is 3%:
$500,000 × 0.03 = $15,000 Commission.

Common Commission Rates

  • Real Estate: Typically 3% to 6% of the final sale price.
  • SaaS Sales: Usually 10% to 20% of the Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR).
  • Retail: Often between 5% and 15% depending on the product margin.
