How to Calculate Yearly Salary Manually

If you want to do the math yourself, here is the standard formula used by HR departments and Payroll software:

Hourly Rate × Hours per Week × 52 Weeks = Gross Annual Salary

Example: If you make $35 an hour working full-time:
$35 × 40 hours = $1,400 per week.
$1,400 × 52 weeks = $72,800 per year.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Does this calculator include taxes?
    No. This tool calculates your Gross Income (before taxes). To find your "Take Home Pay," you must deduct federal and state taxes, which vary depending on your location and marital status.
  • What is a full-time work year?
    A standard full-time year is considered 2,080 hours. This is calculated as 40 hours per week multiplied by 52 weeks.
  • How do I calculate for Part-Time work?
    Simply adjust the "Hours per week" setting in the calculator. For example, if you work 20 hours a week at $20/hr, your annual income would be $20,800.
