How to Calculate a Discount

Whether you're shopping during a sale or calculating margins for your business, knowing the final price after a percentage off is essential.

The Formula

To find the discounted price, follow these steps:

Savings = Original Price × (Discount Rate / 100)
Final Price = Original Price - Savings

Example Calculation

If a pair of shoes costs $80 and is on sale for 25% off:

  • Step 1: Convert 25% to decimal (0.25).
  • Step 2: Multiply $80 × 0.25 = $20 (Savings).
  • Step 3: Subtract $20 from $80 = $60 (Final Price).
