What is a URL Slug?

A slug is the part of a URL that identifies a specific page on a website in an easy-to-read form. It is the part of the URL that explains the page's content.

Example: omnitools.com/url-slug-generator

Why Clean URLs Matter for SEO

  • Readability: Users should be able to predict the content of a page just by looking at the link.
  • Keywords: Search engines like Google look for keywords in your URL. A slug like /clean-url-tool ranks better than /p=123.
  • Stop Words: Removing common words like "a", "the", and "of" keeps your URL short and keyword-rich, which is an SEO best practice.
