Free CPM Calculator for YouTubers & Bloggers

Whether you are a YouTube Creator, a TikTok Influencer, or a Website Publisher using Google AdSense, understanding your CPM (Cost Per Mille) is the key to maximizing your revenue. Our free calculator helps you estimate exactly how much money you will make based on your view count and CPM rate.

How to Use This Tool

  • Select Platform: Click on YouTube, TikTok, or AdSense to automatically load the average industry CPM for that platform.
  • Enter Views: Input the number of views your video or article received.
  • Adjust CPM: If you know your specific CPM (found in your analytics dashboard), enter it for precise accuracy.

What is CPM?

CPM stands for "Cost Per Mille" (Mille is Latin for Thousand). It represents how much an advertiser pays for every 1,000 impressions (views) of their advertisement.

The Formula: Earnings = (Total Views / 1000) * CPM Rate

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why is YouTube CPM higher than TikTok?
YouTube videos are longer and can show multiple ads (mid-rolls), whereas TikTok videos are short and ads are easily skipped. Advertisers pay more for the deeper engagement on YouTube.

Q: Does CPM vary by country?
Yes, significantly. Traffic from "Tier 1" countries (USA, UK, Canada, Australia) pays 3x-5x more than traffic from other regions due to higher purchasing power.
