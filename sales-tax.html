What is the difference between Sales Tax and VAT?

  • Sales Tax: Collected by the retailer at the point of sale (common in the USA). It is visible to the customer as an extra charge.
  • VAT (Value Added Tax): Collected at every stage of production (common in Europe/UK). It is usually included in the sticker price.
