Gross Margin vs. Markup: What's the Difference?

If you run a Dropshipping store, Amazon FBA business, or a retail shop, knowing your numbers is the difference between going broke and getting rich. Many entrepreneurs confuse "Margin" and "Markup," which can lead to pricing errors.

1. Gross Margin (The "Health" Metric)

Margin is the percentage of the selling price that is profit. If you have a 40% margin, it means you keep $0.40 of every dollar you make.
Formula: (Price - Cost) / Price

2. Markup (The "Pricing" Metric)

Markup is the percentage you add to the cost to get the selling price. If you buy a shirt for $10 and sell it for $15, you marked it up 50%.
Formula: (Price - Cost) / Cost
