Free Paycheck & Salary Calculator (2025 Estimator)

When you receive a job offer for $60,000 a year, you don't actually get $60,000. Taxes, Social Security, and Medicare take a significant bite out of your paycheck. Our Paycheck Calculator helps you visualize exactly how much money will hit your bank account.

How is Net Pay calculated?

Gross Pay: The total salary offered by your employer.
Net Pay: The amount left after deductions.

Net Pay = Gross Pay - (Federal Tax + State Tax + FICA)

This tool allows you to input an estimated "Total Tax Rate" (typically 20-30% for most Americans) to give you a quick, reliable estimate of your spending money.

Hourly vs. Salary

Sometimes it is hard to compare an hourly job to a salaried one.
Rule of Thumb: Take the hourly rate and multiply by 2,000 to get the annual salary (assuming 40 hours/week, 50 weeks/year).
Example: $25/hour is approx $50,000/year.
