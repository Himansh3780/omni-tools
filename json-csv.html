How to Convert JSON to CSV?

JSON (JavaScript Object Notation) is the language of the web APIs, but CSV (Comma Separated Values) is the language of spreadsheets like Microsoft Excel and Google Sheets.

Developers and data analysts often need to bridge this gap. Our tool flattens complex JSON structures into simple rows and columns.

Privacy First: Serverless Conversion

Most online converters upload your sensitive data to their servers to process it. We do not do that. Our converter runs entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your data never leaves your computer, making it safe for corporate or private data.

JSON Structure Requirements

To convert successfully, your JSON should be an Array of Objects. Example:

[
  { "id": 1, "product": "Laptop", "price": 999 },
  { "id": 2, "product": "Phone", "price": 499 }
]

Nested objects will be stringified or flattened depending on complexity.
