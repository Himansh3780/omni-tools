What is Open Graph (OG)?

The Open Graph protocol was introduced by Facebook to allow any webpage to become a rich object in a social graph. Basically, it allows you to control exactly what is displayed when someone shares your link on social media.

Why do you need OG Tags?

  • Higher CTR: A link with a large, beautiful image gets 2-3x more clicks than a plain text link.
  • Professionalism: Broken or missing images on social shares make your brand look amateur.
  • Cross-Platform: While invented by Facebook, these tags are now used by LinkedIn, Pinterest, Discord, and Slack to generate link previews.

Image Size Requirements

For the best results across all platforms, use an image that is 1200 x 630 pixels. This ensures high-resolution display on retina screens and fits the 1.91:1 aspect ratio standard.
