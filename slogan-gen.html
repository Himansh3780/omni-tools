How to Create a Memorable Slogan

A great slogan (or tagline) acts as the "hook" that stays in your customer's mind. Think of Nike's "Just Do It" or Apple's "Think Different." These short phrases encapsulate the entire philosophy of the brand.

3 Rules for a Sticky Tagline

  • Keep it Short: The best slogans are usually 3 to 5 words long.
  • Focus on Benefit: Don't just say what you do; say how it feels. Instead of "We sell fast cars," try "The Ultimate Driving Machine."
  • Be Honest: Ensure your slogan aligns with your actual brand promise.
