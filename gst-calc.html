The Ultimate GST & VAT Calculator (2025 Edition)

Whether you are a freelancer in India, a business owner in the UK, or a startup in Canada, calculating taxes correctly is non-negotiable. Errors in tax calculation can lead to lost revenue or compliance issues.

Our tool is distinct because it offers "Reverse Calculation". Often, you buy a product for $118 and need to know what the original price was before the 18% tax was added. This calculator solves that instantly, showing you the exact breakdown between the Base Amount (Profit) and the Tax Component (Liability).

Mode 1: Adding Tax (Exclusive)

Use this mode when creating an invoice. You have your service fee (e.g., $1000) and need to add 18% GST on top.

Formula: Total = Base + (Base * Rate / 100)

Mode 2: Removing Tax (Inclusive)

Use this mode for expense tracking. You paid a total bill of $1180 and need to separate the tax amount for your filings.

Formula: Base = Total / (1 + Rate/100)

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Is GST and VAT calculated differently?
    No. The math is identical. Whether your country calls it GST (Goods and Services Tax), VAT (Value Added Tax), or HST (Harmonized Sales Tax), the percentage formulas used in this tool are universal.
  • Q: Can I use this for Australian GST (10%)?
    Yes. Simply click the "10%" preset chip or type 10 in the rate box.
  • Q: What is the formula to remove VAT?
    To remove VAT, divide the total amount by 1.xx (where xx is the VAT rate). For example, to remove 20% VAT, divide by 1.20. Our tool automates this instantly.
