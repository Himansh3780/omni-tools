Why Responsive Design Testing Matters

In 2025, over 60% of all web traffic comes from mobile devices. If your website looks broken on an iPhone, you are losing more than half of your potential customers. Google also uses "Mobile-First Indexing," meaning they rank your site based on its mobile version, not the desktop one.

Common Breakpoints

  • Mobile (Portrait): 320px - 480px
  • Tablet (Portrait): 768px - 1024px
  • Laptop: 1366px - 1440px
  • Desktop: 1920px+

How to use this tool

Simply enter your URL above. Our tool simulates the viewport size of popular devices. You can also use the "Rotate" button to switch between Portrait and Landscape modes to check your layout flexibility.
