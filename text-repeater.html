Why use a Text Repeater?

Sometimes you need to make a point, loud and clear. Whether you are sending a heartfelt "I am sorry" 100 times to a friend or spamming a WhatsApp group with funny emojis, typing it manually is impossible.

Popular Uses

  • WhatsApp Bomber: Send a message so long that the "Read More" button appears.
  • Instagram Comments: Boost engagement by commenting multiple times instantly.
  • Testing: Developers use this to generate dummy text to test app performance with large data.

Performance Note

This tool runs entirely in your browser. While we allow up to 10,000 repetitions, be careful! Creating extremely large text blocks (1 million+) might freeze your phone temporarily.
